I study how environmental variation in space and time is buffered or amplified across scales to generate emergent properties of complex ecosystems. I am particularly interested in forecasting population responses to climate change, species coexistence, and the stability of ecosystem functioning at multiple spatial scales. A key approach in all my work is fusing models with data using contemporary statistical approaches, which allows me to gain understanding of the processes that underly observable patterns.
